This page is temporarily closed in a protest against software patents. For websites to be closed down due to software patents could become a reality in the near future. Software patents can get you prosecuted for publishing your own work!

For more information go to swpat.ffii.org. If you wish to enter this site, then click here.

The European Parliament will take a key decision on the proposed directive to change european patent-law now in September -03. This directive will even make the infamous "Amazon one klick" patentable, or the recently uncovered European patent from Amazon on ordering a gift for and automatically delivering it to a third party.

Please contact your european politician, ask him/her to vote against the directive "COD/2002/0047" and not make software patentable.

Software patents:

  • Reduce innovation and increase monopolies, harming consumers choice and value for money.
  • Undermines e-commerce by legalised extortion from patent holders.
  • Jeopardises basic freedom of creation and publication, a patent holder could censor publication by the author of an original program.
  • Cause legal uncertainty to copyright holders through patent inflation, since they won't know they are infringing someone else's patents until blackmailed or sued.
  • Monopolises information (software is only information/instructions).
